# Vijeta
### The Operating System for Ambitious Students

Version: 0.1 (MVP)
Owner: Vedant Talekar

---

# Vision

Every year thousands of talented students never participate in scholarships,
hackathons and competitions because they don't know where to start,
can't find teammates or don't have proper guidance.

Vijeta exists to remove every barrier between
"I want to participate"
and
"I became a winner."

Winning should not be the end of the journey.
Every winner should create resources that help produce the next generation of winners.

This creates a self-sustaining ecosystem.

---

# Problem Statement

Today students face five major problems.

## 1. Discovery

Relevant opportunities are scattered across dozens of websites.

Students miss deadlines.

---

## 2. Team Formation

Many students skip competitions because they cannot find teammates.

---

## 3. Guidance

Even after finding opportunities,
students don't know

- where to begin
- what previous winners built
- how judging works
- what resources are useful

---

## 4. Participation Barrier

Students believe

"I don't know coding."

But competitions require many different skills.

Examples

- Design
- Research
- Documentation
- Presentation
- Marketing
- Testing
- Video Editing
- Product Management

---

## 5. Knowledge Loss

Every winning project disappears after the competition.

Future students start from zero.

# Product Pillars

## 1. Discover

Purpose

Help students discover opportunities.

Supports

- Scholarships
- Hackathons
- Competitions
- Fellowships
- Research Programs
- Internships

Features

- Smart search
- Deadline reminders
- Eligibility filters
- Bookmark opportunities
- Calendar integration

Future

AI recommendations based on profile.

---

## 2. Team Up

Purpose

Reduce participation friction.

Features

Create Team

Need

- AI Engineer
- Frontend
- Backend
- Designer
- Research
- Presenter

Students can

- Join teams
- Apply to teams
- Invite teammates

Matching based on

- Skills
- Interests
- Location
- College
- Experience

Future

AI teammate recommendations.

---

## 3. Guidance Library

Purpose

Learn from previous winners.

Every opportunity contains

- Winning projects
- Winning PPT
- Architecture
- Timeline
- Resources
- FAQs
- Common mistakes
- Mentor notes

AI Features

"Explain this winning project"

"Summarize this repository"

"Generate starter roadmap"

---

## 4. Build Workspace

Purpose

Help teams execute.

Instead of moving to 10 different tools.

Features

- Task board
- Shared notes
- Resource links
- File uploads
- AI project planner
- Timeline tracker

Future

AI project manager.

---

## 5. Showcase

Purpose

Celebrate winners.

Every winner publishes

- Project
- Demo
- PPT
- GitHub
- Architecture
- Learnings
- Resources
- Write-up

Showcase becomes permanent portfolio.

---

## 6. Mentorship (Future)

Experienced winners can mentor newcomers.

Mentorship options

# AI Layer

AI exists to reduce effort.

Not replace students.

How do I start?

Generate roadmap.

Check eligibility.

Review presentation.

Review resume.

Review SOP.

Review documentation.

Generate checklist.

---

### AI Knowledge Extraction

Every uploaded project automatically generates

- Summary

- Lessons learned
- FAQs
